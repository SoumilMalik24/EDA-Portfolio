##  Highlight: FIFA 21 Analysis
*> A deep dive into cleaning messy real-world data and understanding the football player market.*

In this flagship project, I tackled a raw dataset of 19,000+ players containing mixed units, currency strings, and formatting errors. 
- **The Challenge:** Converting messy strings like `"€100.5M"` and `"5'9"` into usable numerical data.
- **The Insight:** Discovered that a player's **"Reactions"** stat (0.87 correlation) is a stronger predictor of market value than physical speed or strength.
